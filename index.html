Who we are
Built on Trust, Delivered with Craft

Gratia Construction has been a trusted name in Zimbabwe's construction industry for over a decade. Based in Masvingo, we combine hands-on expertise with modern techniques to deliver residential, commercial, and civil projects that stand the test of time.


Our team of engineers, surveyors, and skilled tradespeople approach every build with the same commitment — do it right, do it safely, and deliver beyond expectations.
